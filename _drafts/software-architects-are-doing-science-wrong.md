It is no secret that my role in the cybersecurity industry is best described as skeptical respect. We respect each other, but there is plenty of skepticism.

This weekend I finally recognized what the crux of our philosophical difference is, and it is one of how the two schools of thought approach the scientific method.

The method itself that we both follow is identical. Our first-principles are the same. Our deductive methodologies are the same. Our deductions would never disagree.

What we disagree about are the variables we can control, and those we cannot.

This is also the basic process of learning. This is how evolution works, this is how intelligence learns, and this is what all of AI is based on. If all you understood was, iterative error reduction, you just learned all of the hype behind Deep Learning, and Multi-layer Perceptrons, Convolutional neural networks, etc. etc.
