Given that everyone is AI now here’s my sanity checklist I vet any AI promise or pitch against.

#### AI cannot create new information

I mean information in the [very physical existance of the Universe sense](https://arxiv.org/abs/0708.2837).

AI certainly cannot make up information that does not exist, but furthermore, AI cannot divine information that’s not present in the training data, even if it were to exist elsewhere.
