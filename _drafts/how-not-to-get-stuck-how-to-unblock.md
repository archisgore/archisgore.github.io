This post is for programmers, developers, coders, system administrators, etc. but a lot of these guidelines should apply anywhere else.

Literally the most helpless you’ll ever feel when programming, running something or trying something is when you just don’t know what’s going on. What you do know is “it’s not working” or alternatively, “I don’t know how to know that it’s not working. Maybe it is working. What’s going on here?”
