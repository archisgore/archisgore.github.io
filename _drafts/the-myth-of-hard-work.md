My first ever manager said something to me that has impacted everything in my life. I used to work nights and weekends, I would even sleep at the office. I was hungry, ambitious and I wanted to achieve.

My manager at the time said to me, “*If you work twice as hard as an SDE, I’ll happily pay you twice that much of an SDE. But if you want to be promoted to a Senior SDE, you need to do different work, not more work.*”

That once sentence gave me a perspective that continues to pay off every single day.
