### The improbable founding of SpaceX

It’s May 5th, 2002. [Elon Musk](https://twitter.com/elonmusk) has hired a bunch of the top rocket scientists in the world, paying them with his personal money. Let’s recap the conversation the night before [SpaceX was founded](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SpaceX#History)..

> Elon: Hey guys. I have an idea. This is about to sound wild! Crazy even. I might sound like I’m out of my mind.

> Scientists: Oh okay… well you are paying us a lot… so go for it!

> Elon: But seriously. I know you might think I’m joking with you. I’m not. Literally nobody has thought of this before. I know I’ve not been involved in any of NASA’s projects personally. I don’t know all that they know. Maybe I’m completely off on this.

> Scientists: It’s okay… we’re being paid. Just come out with it.

> Elon: Alright… here goes. Nobody laugh. I know nobody’s ever thought of this one before…. but… okay… and I’m completely serious… but… What if we could refuel the same rocket multiple times and fly it?

> Scientists: \*In collective gasp\* OH…. MY… GOD!!!!!! DAMN! Darn!! How has nobody thought of this before? Damnn… we’re speechless. Woe is upon us. A century of rocket science and we just never thought of this one simple magical concept. Elon, just how do you do it? How do you think of the one key idea nobody has ever thought of? Holy cow! We MUST protect this above all else! If only this idea spread…. damn… the world of Rocket Science would change for ever. Wow!!!! Can we pay you lots of money for this? Damn. We feel like we owe you millions of dollars for coming up with this!

This conversation is ludicrous and utterly stupid. If this really happened on the night before SpaceX’s founding, you might want to sell that stock today.

### Cars that promise to run on water

If you’re a product owner, product manager, investor, venture capitalist, etc., then this is your nightmare scenario. This is Theranos.

A proposal comes across your desk. There’s an idea. A huge idea. If built correctly, this idea will literally make one trillion dollars. I’m not joking. I don’t mean 999 billion dollars. I mean literally one trillion dollars. Not a dollar less.

The proposer is asking you for only a one percent stake in this idea. But they’re asking for that one percent upfront. It may be in the form of a promotion. It may be in the form of cash. It’s the idea that matters, afterall.

Don’t worry — you’ll be provided data, projections, user studies, user scenarios, etc. etc. This is data-driven. This idea is market-proven. It cannot fail. The person who proposed this will give you the entire investment back, if the idea does not literally make one trillion dollars in cold hard cash!

The freakin Federal Reserve is going to have to run their minting presses overtime just to create enough cash in the world!

Now here’s the idea….. are you ready for it?

“Tell your Engineering Team to build a car that runs on water!”

Boom! Mic Drop! That’s all she wrote! Come back when it happens, and be prepared for literal truck loads of shipments in dollar bills!

These cars are all around us. Everywhere. You’re being pitched one right now. Whether it is the latest breakthrough in hair growth, or the fat loss pill, or something that promises to detect any disease using a single drop of blood.

### Why you should care…

Before we proceed, why should we even care? Didn’t Theranos pay for their sins? Aren’t they being prosecuted? Is there any damage except to the investors and Elizabeth Holmes?

A lighthearted study of history will show us we should care, and we should have cared a great deal more than we did.
