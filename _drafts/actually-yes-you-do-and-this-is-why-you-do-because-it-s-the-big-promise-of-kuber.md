Actually yes you do. And this is why you do: because it’s the big promise of Kubernetes.

The whole pitch of Kubernetes’ complexity is that it makes everything else simple.

If something is good and necessary, the right reaction is to ask for more tools to make I easier. I would flip this argument: You do need HA control planes. Ask your Kubernetes vendor, or let’s go fix the design to make running that a lot easier.
