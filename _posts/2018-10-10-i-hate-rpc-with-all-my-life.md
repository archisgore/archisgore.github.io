I HATE RPC with all my life. Have done so for about a decade. Your post made me LOVE RPC. This is exactly the RPC to convert me! Solves the boilerplate code writing problems. Maintains the benefits of everything else that is well understood and allowing me to control it — DNS lookups, load balancing, routing, etc. etc. — heck I can have an intermediate router that can path-based route sub-requests as I migrate a service without the client knowing. And not losing back-compat with clients who want to hand-write HTTP+JSON if the wished.
