Most of you know that I spent a small fortune [reacquiring my patent](https://assignment.uspto.gov/patent/index.html#/patent/search/resultAbstract?id=10733303&type=patNum) that was owned by my former employer.

Today I’m pledging that patent into the public domain i.e. I will not prosecute any infringements, and to make it more concrete, for a nominal fee to my contracting lawyers, will grant anyone who asks, a perpetual unlimited unrestricted license to use it.

I also hastily wrote a whitepaper hosted on [encrypted-execution.com](https://encrypted-execution.com) that explains what the patent is about, and what I want to do with it.

I’m going to put up development bounties for developers who can build some of the stuff that I don’t have time for and legally shouldn’t. Your contributions will all be open source and free for all to use as they see fit.

More updates once I get some time off to play with compilers.
